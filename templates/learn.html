Pangram Solution: UNBOXING

The pangram is "UNBOXING" - the process of taking something out of its box.

How to approach solving this:

  1. Look for common word endings: -ING is a very productive suffix
  2. Consider prefixes like UN- that can combine with verbs
  3. Try combinations: UN + BOX + ING = UNBOXING
